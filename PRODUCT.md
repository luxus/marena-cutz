# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Marena Cutz serves clients in and around Bülach who want a precise haircut, clean fade, beard service, or style change from a modern local barber. Visitors usually arrive from social media, search, or referrals and want to understand the vibe, prices, location, opening hours, and booking path quickly.

## Product Purpose

The website should convert interest into bookings while making the salon feel skilled, current, and trustworthy. Success means visitors can get a clear price impression, recognize the craft level, find the shop, and book without friction.

## Brand Personality

Street precision: sharp, confident, craft-first. The tone should feel modern and direct without becoming corporate, vintage, or generic tech-neon.

## Anti-references

Do not look like a corporate salon, spa, or stock wellness template. Avoid vintage barbershop clichés such as beige palettes, retro serifs, moustache marks, and old-school badges. Avoid generic AI neon blue-on-black styling that feels auto-generated.

## Design Principles

- Lead with craft: show the cut, the tools, the chair, and the result before explaining too much.
- Keep decisions fast: prices, booking, location, hours, and socials must be easy to scan.
- Use edge without cosplay: sharp and street-aware, but not nightclub loud or cyberpunk default.
- Local trust beats polish theatre: make Marena Cutz feel real, nearby, and bookable.
- Details signal quality: typography, spacing, icon choices, and image treatment should feel intentional.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for accessible contrast, readable type sizes, keyboard-friendly links, and reduced reliance on color alone. Motion should remain subtle and non-essential.
